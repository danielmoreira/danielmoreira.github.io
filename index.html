Welcome!

My name is Daniel and I am a computer scientist. I am currently an assistant research professor at the University of Notre Dame, US, and I received my PhD degree from the University of Campinas, Brazil, in 2016.

As a member of the Computer Vision Research Lab, I investigate the application of techniques from the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Visual Media Forensics to make our society better.

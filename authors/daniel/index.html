Daniel H. Moreira

Postdoctoral Researcher

University of Notre Dame

My name is Daniel and I am a computer scientist. I received my PhD degree from the University of Campinas, Brazil, in 2016, and I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Notre Dame, US.

As a member of the Computer Vision Research Lab, I investigate the application of techniques from the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Visual Media Forensics to make our society better.
